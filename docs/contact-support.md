# KickChasers Contact and Support

**Effective Date:** March 1, 2026

KickChasers wants to provide helpful and practical support to players, clubs, coaches, trackers, and families using the app.

## 1. How to contact us

For general support, account help, bug reports, privacy questions, or moderation concerns, contact:

**Email:** kickchasersad@gmail.com  
**Website:** www.kickchasers.com  
**Address:** 33a Curzon St, Camden Park SA 5038, Australia

## 2. What support we can help with

We can help with issues such as:
- account access problems
- login or verification issues
- app bugs or crashes
- profile or team setup issues
- game tracking problems
- incorrect or missing content
- report, block, or moderation concerns
- privacy or data access requests
- general product questions

## 3. Reporting a problem

When contacting support, it helps if you include:
- your device type
- operating system
- app version
- a description of the issue
- screenshots if relevant
- the game, post, team, or profile involved if relevant

This helps us investigate faster.

## 4. Response expectations

We will aim to respond within a reasonable timeframe, but response times may vary depending on the nature of the issue and support volume.

Safety, privacy, and account access issues may be prioritised.

## 5. Moderation and safety issues

If you believe content or user behavior on KickChasers breaks the rules, you should use the in-app reporting tools where available.

For urgent moderation or safety issues, you can also contact us directly at kickchasersad@gmail.com.

## 6. Account and privacy requests

If you want to:
- access your personal information
- correct account details
- request deletion of your account
- raise a privacy concern

please contact us at kickchasersad@gmail.com.

We may need to verify your identity before actioning certain requests.

## 7. Product feedback

We welcome product feedback and suggestions. While we cannot promise every request will be implemented, feedback helps shape future updates to KickChasers.
