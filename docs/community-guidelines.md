# KickChasers Community Guidelines

**Effective Date:** March 1, 2026

KickChasers is built for players, clubs, coaches, trackers, families, and supporters of grassroots Australian Rules Football. We want the platform to be competitive, useful, and respectful.

These Community Guidelines explain what is and is not acceptable on KickChasers.

## 1. Be respectful

Treat others with respect. Healthy competition is welcome. Abuse is not.

Do not:
- harass
- bully
- threaten
- humiliate
- intimidate
- target other users unfairly

## 2. No hate or discrimination

Do not post content that attacks or demeans people based on attributes such as race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, gender, sexuality, disability, or age.

## 3. Keep it appropriate

KickChasers is a sports platform. Do not post:
- sexually explicit content
- graphic violence
- obscene or offensive material
- content that is inappropriate for a football community environment

## 4. No spam or misleading content

Do not:
- post repeated unwanted content
- mislead users
- impersonate individuals or clubs
- use the platform primarily for irrelevant promotion or spam

## 5. Respect privacy

Only share content, photos, stats, and personal information you are entitled to share.

Be especially careful with information relating to junior players. Do not publish private or sensitive personal information about others without permission.

## 6. Keep competition fair

Do not deliberately misuse KickChasers features to create false data, fake profiles, manipulated stats, misleading rankings, or deceptive match content.

## 7. Use reporting tools responsibly

If you see content that breaks the rules, use the report feature.

Do not abuse reporting tools to target or harass other users.

## 8. Blocking and personal boundaries

If another user is bothering you or making your experience uncomfortable, use the block feature.

## 9. What happens if rules are broken

If content or conduct violates these Guidelines or our Terms, we may take action such as:
- removing content
- restricting features
- warning users
- suspending accounts
- permanently removing accounts

## 10. Help us keep the platform safe

KickChasers works best when clubs, players, families, and supporters use it responsibly. Please help keep the platform positive, competitive, and respectful.

If you need help, contact us at kickchasersad@gmail.com.
