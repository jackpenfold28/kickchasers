# KickChasers Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 1, 2026

KickChasers respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how KickChasers collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the KickChasers website, mobile app, and related services.

If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use KickChasers.

## 1. Who we are

KickChasers is a grassroots Australian Rules Football platform that helps players, clubs, coaches, trackers, and families record live match data, view statistics, manage squads, follow teams, and share match-related content.

## 2. Information we collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

### Account and profile information
When you create or use an account, we may collect information such as:
- your name
- email address
- username or handle
- password or authentication details
- date of birth
- profile photo
- action photo
- club, squad, role, and player details
- jersey number and playing position

### App activity and usage information
When you use KickChasers, we may collect:
- match tracking activity
- player statistics
- lineup selections
- game summaries
- leaderboard data
- likes, follows, comments, and other in-app interactions
- content you create or share
- report and block actions
- settings and preferences

### Device and technical information
We may collect technical data such as:
- device type
- operating system
- app version
- IP address
- crash logs
- diagnostics
- push notification token
- general usage analytics

### Support and communication information
If you contact us, report a problem, or make a support request, we may collect:
- your email address
- the contents of your message
- screenshots, attachments, or logs you provide
- any related account or app information needed to help you

## 3. How we use your information

We use your information to:
- create and manage your account
- provide app features and services
- track games and record statistics
- display profiles, squads, teams, posts, comments, and leaderboards
- personalize your experience
- send notifications, including game-related and social notifications
- improve app performance and reliability
- investigate bugs, crashes, and technical issues
- enforce our Terms of Service and Community Guidelines
- review reports and moderate content
- respond to support requests
- protect users, the app, and our systems from abuse or misuse
- comply with legal obligations

## 4. User-generated content

KickChasers includes features that allow users to create or contribute content, including:
- player profiles
- team and squad information
- match data and statistics
- posts and comments
- social cards and match content

Content you choose to publish or share through the app may be visible to other users depending on the feature and its visibility settings. Please only share content you are comfortable making available through the platform.

## 5. Notifications

If you allow notifications, KickChasers may send you push notifications about:
- followed clubs or squads
- game starts and live activity
- comments, follows, and social interactions
- account or moderation activity
- important app updates

You can manage notification preferences through your device settings and, where available, within the app.

## 6. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information:
- with service providers that help operate the app, such as hosting, database, analytics, crash reporting, and push notification providers
- when necessary to investigate safety, security, fraud, or abuse
- when required by law, regulation, court order, or legal process
- in connection with a business restructure, sale, or transfer of assets, if that ever occurs

We may also share content and information you deliberately make visible through public or social features of the app.

## 7. Data storage and security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss, and unauthorized access, modification, or disclosure.

However, no platform or transmission method is completely secure. You use KickChasers at your own risk, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Data retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to:
- provide the service
- maintain records
- resolve disputes
- enforce our terms
- comply with legal obligations

We may delete or anonymize information when it is no longer needed.

## 9. Blocking, reporting, and moderation

KickChasers allows users to:
- report posts and comments
- block other users

We may review content, reports, and related account information to enforce our rules and maintain safety on the platform. We may remove content or restrict accounts where appropriate.

## 10. Children and young users

KickChasers may be used by junior players and families. If you are under 18, you should use KickChasers with the involvement of a parent, guardian, club, or responsible adult where appropriate.

Parents, guardians, coaches, and clubs should be mindful when uploading player information, photos, or statistics relating to young people.

If you believe a child’s personal information has been used inappropriately on KickChasers, please contact us at kickchasersad@gmail.com.
